Website Project Plan: Impressions of Light

Gary Guymon's Impressions of Light

Site Name

Impressions of Light: This name reflects both the Impressionist style and how my dad's paintings capture the beauty of light and landscapes inspired by the Blanding, Utah area.

Site Purpose

The site will showcase my dad's collection of Impressionist art, which portrays the beauty of southeastern Utah. His work highlights local landscapes, rural life, and the rich history of the region. This includes scenes inspired by Native American tribes and pioneers who shaped the area. The site will feature:

  • A gallery of paintings categorized by themes such as landscapes, rural life, and historical heritage.
  • An 'About the Artist' page sharing his journey, artistic inspiration, and experiences.
  • A contact form for inquiries regarding commissions or artwork requests.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find paintings inspired by the landscapes near Blanding, UT?
  • How can I contact the artist to request a commission or learn more about his work?
  • What inspires the artist to portray the cultural heritage of Native American tribes and pioneers?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Dark Warm Gray (#2E2E2E) providing high contrast and sophistication.
Secondary Color: Slate Gray (#5A5A5A) for balance, echoing the muted tones of shadow and stone.
Neutral Color 1: Soft White (#F5F5F5) for background contrast and readability.
Neutral Color 2: Forest Greenish Brown (#2E4239) reflecting natural foliage and earthy elements.
Accent Color: Warm Crimson (#7A2D35) adding vibrancy and warmth inspired by highlights in landscape art.

Typography

Heading Font: Playfair Display (serif) an elegant, artistic font for headers.
Body Font: Lato (sans-serif) clean and readable for body text.
Accent Font: Dancing Script used for quotes or artistic captions.
